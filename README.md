## Service 2: UI / UX Localization

**Best for:** Mobile games, Battle passes, Multiplayer menus.

### The Problem:
English text often expands or contracts in other languages.
- German: "Settings" (9 chars) → "Einstellungen" (13 chars) – breaks button layouts
- Chinese: "Health" (6 chars) → "生命值" (3 chars) – leaves weird empty spaces

### What we do:
- Resize buttons & text boxes (we provide CSS notes for your developer)
- Translate menu tabs (Inventory, Map, Skills, Party)
- Localize date/time formats (MM/DD vs DD/MM vs YYYY/MM/DD)
- Convert currency symbols ($ → € → ¥)

## Service 3: Cultural Adaptation (Localization vs. Literal)

**Best for:** Games with humor, religious themes, or real-world references.

### Examples of our changes:

| Original (English) | Adapted (Japanese) | Reason |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| "Holy Grail" | "Legendary Chalice" | Avoids direct Christian symbolism |
| "Pork chop" (food item) | "Beef steak" | Avoids pork in Muslim-majority regions |
| "That's what she said" joke | Rewritten punchline | Doesn't work in Spanish or Korean |

**Our Promise:** *"We don't change your story—we make your story make sense in another culture."*
